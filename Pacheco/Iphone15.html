Apple's 'Wonderlust' Event Highlights the iPhone 15 Series

At its annual fall event, Apple launched its latest round of iPhones.
And yes, USB-C has landed on the iPhone 15.

The iPhone 15 series was officially announced by Apple on September 12, 2023. The announcement event, called the "Wonderlust" event,
unveiled several models in the series including:

  1. iPhone 15
  2. iPhone 15 Plus
  3. iPhone 15 Pro
  4. iPhone 15 Pro Max

These new iPhone models came with various improvements and features over their predecessors, including camera innovations, better battery life,
improved performance, and updated designs.

Following the announcement, pre-orders for the iPhone 15 models started on September 15, 2023, and the devices became available for purchase
and shipping on September 22, 2023 for over 40 countries including the US, the UK, India, Germany, Japan, and China.

For more information about the event click here: Wonderlust event

New Camera. New design. Newphoria.

iPhone 15 and 15 Plus inherit a lot from the iPhone 14 Pro, minus the telephoto camera, including the Dynamic Island widget
that expands the camera cutout to show contextual information. It also has a new 48MP camera that gives a super-high-resolution photos.
The 15 Plus has a bigger battery and a 6.7-inch screen compared to the 6.1-inch of the 15. Both also use the second-generation
Ultra Wideband chip that's in the new watches.


The iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus has five color options:

  • pink
  • yellow
  • green
  • blue
  • black
The storage capacities of iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus are as follows:
  • 128GB
  • 256GB
  • 512GB

iPhone 15 Pro

Titanium. So strong. So light. So Pro.

Introducing the first iPhone to have a titanium alloy case with an aluminum substructure, iPhone 15 Pro.
The aerospace-grade titanium design makes it lighter (though not thinner) and better for heat dissipation, and has shrunken borders
for more screen visibility. The Pro screen size are 6.1 inches for Pro and 6.7 inches for Pro Max. Their new internal design
also improves repairability, partly because the back glass can be replaced.

On the other hand, the iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max are offered in colors:

  • black titanium
  • white titanium
  • blue titanium
  • natural titanium

Also the iPhone 15 Pro gets an additional 1TB storage option, while the iPhone 15 Pro Max comes storage capacities in:

  • 256GB
  • 512GB
  • 1TB
