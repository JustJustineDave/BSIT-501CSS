THE NEW IPHONE IS HERE!

Apple's consumer-focused flagship models, the iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus ,
start at $799. The professional-oriented iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone Pro Max,
which start at $999 and have more features and capabilities, are added to the group.
he iPhone 15 family consists of a total of four models,including two 6.1-inch iPhones and two 6.7-inch iPhones,
