iPhone 15 vs Iphone 15 pro

  • iPhone 15 Pro price starts at $1,000, while iPhone 15 starts at $800

  • Both have 6.1" screens, but iPhone 15 Pro has:
    • Slimmer bezels

  • iPhone 15 Pro has a 3X zoom camera
  • iPhone 15 Pro has slightly longer battery life

  • iPhone 15 Pro supports faster transfer rates
  • iPhone 15 Pro has an Action Button
